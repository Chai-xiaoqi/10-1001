Technology Automation

What is techonoly automation?

Technology automation is the creation and application of technologies to produce and deliver goods and services with minimum human inventions. It is now very common to see the application of automation in the workplace. A study shows that while few occupations are fully automatable, 60% of all occupations have at least 30% technically automatable activities. For example, we use computers to enable more efficient processing of job applicants and employee selection, or even to monitor job performance. While automation creates lots of benefits to our life, it also brings us some difficulties. Our topic is to investigate how automation affects our society both positively and negatively.

Why is it important?

Because nowadays automation is commonly utilized in our society and it does bring us some difficulties besides all the benefits. Therefore, it is important for us to find out and to understand the potential impacts and to see if we can improve some of the defects.
